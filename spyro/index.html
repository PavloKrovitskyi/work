Zumba

Zumba

Zumba is a complete, licensed fitness program that combines elements of cardio, muscle conditioning, balance, and flexibility. The choreography is mainly inspired by Latin dances such as salsa, merengue, cumbia, reggaeton, soca, samba, tango, and flamenco but also incorporates elements of belly dance and Quebradita ³. Zumba is a dynamic dance workout that effectively burns calories and has a positive impact on both the mind and body ⁴. It's a fun way to forget about the daily routine and release your inner energy!

Yoga

Yoga

Yoga improves flexibility, increasing the range of movement in tight places. It also helps strengthen the muscles and improve the balance. Yoga maintains joint health, facilitates and prevents back pain, and teaches better breathing. In addition, yoga promotes mental rest, reduces stress and reduces the physical consequences of stress on the body. It also relieves pain and improves lung function, encouraging relaxation through respiratory practices known as pranayama.

Martial Arts

Martial Arts

Martial arts have many positive health effects. They improve cardiovascular health, increase flexibility, help lose weight and control blood pressure. Martial arts also improve reflexes and help to achieve a general healthy lifestyle. In addition, they can reduce stress and anxiety, promote the development of self -esteem and emotional stability.
